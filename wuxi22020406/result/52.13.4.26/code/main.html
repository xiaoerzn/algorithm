Frequently Asked Questions
How long does my INRIX IQ trial last?
Upon signing up for INRIX IQ you’ll have a 14-day period to try out our powerful suite of analytics and digitization tools.
What is INRIX IQ?
INRIX IQ is the home for all of INRIX’s powerful insight and data management tools. This includes access to a growing list of mobility intelligence applications and our Data Showcase application that allows you to explore the data used to power INRIX solutions.
What happens after my trial expires?
At the end of your 14-day trial you’ll continue to have access to Data Showcase for several geographies but your access to other modules will end. If you’d like to continue using a product and see the full scope of what it can do, please click “subscribe” and contact an expert on the INRIX team to get access.
How do I get access to one or more INRIX products for my team and I?
Click the “subscribe” button and select the monthly or annual enterprise plan you’re interested in learning more about. An INRIX expert will be happy to walk you through the options we have for teams to use our products.
What functionality is included during the trial period?
Data Showcase module, and limited geographies and/or date ranges available to explore our suite of mobility intelligence applications.
